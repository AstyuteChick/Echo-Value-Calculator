Character Levelling Guide

Ever feel the rush of pulling an early 5 star, only to feel the dread of having to build them?

Or maybe you have a backlog of characters that you wish to build but never get around to doing it… because either you would rather spend waveplates to try and fish for better Echoes (dopamine driven), or the task of investing so much time and energy (or even thinking about everything that you have to do) seems too daunting (resistance driven).

There are so many things to level up, so many things to farm, to finally get your characters “ready.” Here is a list of things you need to do before you can actually start to play them:

  • Level up the character

  • Level up their weapon

  • Unlock all their Forte nodes

  • Level up Forte abilities

  • Level up Echoes (passable enough for overworld)

  • Optimize Echo substats (till passable enough for ToA)

The goal of this guide is to provide an optimal way to build your characters. Here optimal not only means that your characters will be built in the shortest time possible, but it also means that the waveplates are being spent as efficiently as possible and your characters are playable without being fully built as soon as possible.

Character Building Sequence:

Follow these steps sequentially to level up and start using your characters as soon as possible:

  1. Level up your character level (to level 80/80) and weapon as much as you already can with your current resources: If you can take your weapon to level 90 already, great! You can skip to step 3. Do not upgrade your character beyond level 80/80 just yet especially if you’re short on resources.

  2. Weapon to Level 90: In most cases, the return of investment in weapons is unparallelled as compared to any other type of upgrades you can do.

  3. Character to Level 80/80: Don’t ascend your character just yet.

  4. Unlock all Forte Nodes:

    1. Start by unlocking the full middle branch (inherent Forte skills).

    2. Then unlock the entire second level (1 level above Forte abilities).

    3. Then unlock the side branches fully (Crit Stats 2 levels above Forte abilities).

    4. Finally, unlock the remaining nodes (2nd and 4th branch, second level).

  5. Level Forte Talents: Level up your character’s main damaging abilities (or main Forte Talents) to level 8 and secondary (damaging/main) abilities to level 6.

  6. Farm “Base-Level” Echo-set, do NOT min-max yet: It’s important to not spend too much time in this stage. Just put any passable Echoes without worrying about substats, or use the EVC to get a score of “Base-Level” or “Decent” and above. This happens naturally when you start levelling your pieces, as you inevitably need to level up some Echoes to level 25 to rule a judgement on them. However, rather than discarding Echoes that don’t end up being double crit, or unacceptable in other ways, just equip them for now. Remember that the goal is to have fully levelled Echoes (even though only 1 or 2 of the 5 Echoes may be worth keeping), even if they aren’t great, at this stage. Your character is now playable!

  7. Ascend the Character to 80/90: Don’t yet level them up to level 90 just yet, especially if short on shell credits.

  8. Max-level your Forte Talents: Level your main Forte abilities to level 10, secondary abilities to level 8, and the rest of the abilities to level 6.

  9. Farm “Decent” to “Well-Built” Echo-set: Try to reach a build score of around 55 to 66. Specifically, you’re trying to make sure your character has the required amount of ER, and you have 70 or more effective crit rate (generally speaking).

  10. Level Character to 90/90: Especially if the character has the main-DPS role.

  11. Set Echo grinding goals: This is the final phase where you grind for optimized Echo substats. If you have further characters to build, do not get stuck griding endlessly in this stage. Set a goal, and once reached, move on to the next character. The goal can be in terms of certain crit stats or any other metric of your choosing, but the EVC scores provide fully unambiguous assessments and make it very easy to set goals in terms of a precise build score or build tier.

Note that after Step 6, you can already start using your character while you are continuing to upgrade them in the next steps.
