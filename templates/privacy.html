Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2026

Who operates this website

Echo Value Calculator is operated by AstyuteChick (echovaluecalc@gmail.com).

What data is used

This website does not require accounts and does not ask for names, addresses, passwords, or other directly identifying personal information.

Calculator inputs, such as character choices, team choices, Echo stats, Energy Regen values, scores, and error results, may be processed to show results, improve the calculator, understand general usage trends, and detect bugs.

Google Analytics and cookies

This website uses Google Analytics through Google Tag Manager, but only if you allow analytics cookies in the cookie preferences.

Google Analytics helps collect usage information that does not directly identify you, such as page views, approximate location, device/browser information, website interactions, and calculator usage events.

This helps me understand which calculators are useful, find errors, and improve the website. You can change your analytics cookie choice through the cookie preferences.

Learn more about how Google uses information from websites that use Google services: Google Privacy & Terms .

Local storage

This website uses your browser's local storage to remember simple preferences, such as light mode or dark mode. This information stays on your device unless you clear your browser data.

Hosting and server logs

This website is hosted on PythonAnywhere. Like most hosting providers, PythonAnywhere may process technical information needed to deliver the website, maintain security, and diagnose errors, such as request times, requested pages, IP addresses, browser information, and server logs.

For more information, see PythonAnywhere's Privacy and Cookies Policy .

External links and donations

This website may link to external websites, such as the project's GitHub repository or Google privacy information pages. External websites have their own privacy policies.

This website may include a Ko-fi donation button or widget. If you choose to donate, Ko-fi and its payment providers process the payment details. Echo Value Calculator does not directly process payment information.

Advertising and affiliate links

This website currently does not use advertising or affiliate links. If ads or affiliate links are added in the future, this policy will be updated.

Your rights

If you are in the European Economic Area or another region with data protection rights, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data. Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time.

To make a privacy-related request, contact echovaluecalc@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the website changes, especially if new analytics, advertising, affiliate, account, or donation features are added.
