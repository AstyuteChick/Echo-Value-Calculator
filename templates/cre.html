Crit Ratio Equivalency

Critical hits work similarly in most games, and the generally accepted crit ratio for optimal DPS is the 1:2 ratio for critical rate and critical damage respectively. However, the critical damage stat that is used to draw this conclusion is a percentage increase (as it works in Genshin Impact) as opposed to a critical damage multiplier that is seen on the stat screen in Wuthering Waves.

The point of this section is to show that these two stats are the exact same thing and that you can convert between the two by adding or subtracting 100, and therefore you can apply the optimal crit ratio logic here as well.

In mathematical terms, we want to show the following:

\[{CD}_{WW} - 100 = {CD}_{GI},\ \]

where \({CD}_{WW}\) is the crit damage as seen in Wuthering Waves and \({CD}_{GI}\) is the crit damage that is seen in Genshin Impact.

By definition, \({CD}_{GI}\) is a percentage increase in damage. If you have 150% \({CD}_{GI}\), your damage increases by 150%. If your normal hits do 75 damage, 150% of 75 is 112.5, meaning you will do 75 + 112.5 = 187.5 damage on critical hits. In general, for X damage on normal hits, your crit hits will do \(X + X*{CD}_{GI}\) damage.

By definition, \({CD}_{WW}\) is a damage multiplier. If you have 250% \({CD}_{WW}\), your damage is multiplied by 250%. If your normal hits do 75 damage, 250% of 75 is 187.5, which is the damage you’ll do on critical hits, the same as above. In general, for X damage on normal hits, your crit hits do \(X*{CD}_{WW}\).

To prove this in general with the above definitions, we consider a scenario with a normal hit damage of X, and a critical hit damage of Y. We then define both versions of Crit Damage using the same scenario and the above definitions:

\[\frac{Y - X}{X}*100 = {CD}_{GI};\ \frac{Y}{X}*100 = {CD}_{WW}\]

Making Y the subject in both equations and then equating them, we have:

\[\frac{{CD}_{GI}}{100}*X + X = \frac{{CD}_{WW}}{100}*X \rightarrow \frac{{CD}_{GI}}{100} + 1 = \frac{{CD}_{WW}}{100}\]

\[\rightarrow {CD}_{GI} + 100 = {CD}_{WW}\]

proving the above conjecture.

With this, we can modify the known result of the optimal crit ratio for Wuthering Waves by simply converting it to the ones used in games like Genshin Impact. The ratio of crit rate with (crit damage - 100) should be 1:2, with optimal ranges being 1:1.6 to 1:2.2 (also a previously well-known result).
