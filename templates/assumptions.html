Assumptions

These should be fully obvious to everyone and almost everyone’s built characters should automatically fit into these assumptions, but these are stated explicitly here.

The quality of Echoes is determined by its substats. The scores obtained from the EVC may not reflect the actual strength/quality of your build if there are issues of this type (with short examples):

  1. Wrong weapons: For e.g. using Cartethyia’s signature weapon on Camellya.

  2. Wrong mainstats: For e.g. running a 41111 setup (or any setup that’s not within around 5% DPS range of the optimal one). Or for e.g. equipping Def% 1 costs on Carlotta.

  3. Under-levelling: For e.g. having any of the following not max levelled: weapon, character, fortes, and echoes.

  4. Over 100% effective Crit Rate: If you have this, just subtract this additional amount when using the calculator wherever appropriate.

  5. Non-sense Crit Ratio: For e.g. having 26% Crit Rate and 365% Crit Damage.

  6. Wrong use of character/non-sense rotation: Anything that affects the damage profile (how much of the total damage is basic, heavy, skill or liberation) of a character significantly from the norm. For e.g. playing Zani without using her Liberation, or Camellya with only her liberation, in a (bizarre) rotation. For characters with different playstyles (like Encore as hypercarry vs Encore in quickswap with Changli), choose the correct version if available.

Of course, having any of these issues will render the analysis of the builds ineffective, but 99.9% of the players should easily fit in these assumptions because they are either obvious or are automatically taken care of by simply following any guides on YouTube or from websites like Prydwen.

NOTE: EVC handles Energy Regen% (ER%) completely! No extra assumptions are required for this, as long as you follow the instructions when using the EVC.
