Goal

  • The Echo Value Calculator (EVC) assigns a precise value on a consistent scale to an Echo and/or an Echo set (build) that reflects its quality and strength – called the Echo/Build Score.

  • It considers all the factors that practically affect this score – so the score is robust, reliable, and trustworthy.

  • The EVC is quick and easy to use, and aids on-the-fly decision making for users by being as frictionless as possible, without sacrificing previous goals.
