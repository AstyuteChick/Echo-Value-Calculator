What this tool is for

This website is meant to help players estimate and compare Echo, Build, and Full scores. The results are intended for fun, convenience, and general guidance.

I try to make the calculations as useful and accurate as I can, but the results may not always perfectly reflect in-game performance, optimal builds, or future game changes.

Use your own judgement

The calculator can be a very helpful reference when data is entered correctly, instructions are followed correctly, and assumptions are kept in the back of the mind (You can find a list of assumptions under Articles -> Assumptions (www.echovaluecalc.com/assumptions)).

For details on how the calculators works, please find the relevant articles under the "Echo Value Calculator - Theory", found on the Articles page (www.echovaluecalc.com/articles)
