Resonance Cost, Outros and ER requirements

The total ER on the builds should be recalibrated with the outro ER buffs. The most common example is probably Zhezhi with Carlotta.

This part is speculative, but the idea is that during a rotation, the character needs to generate a certain amount of Resonance Energy particles to cast their Liberation, called the Resonance Cost. (For Carlotta, her Liberation has the Resonance cost of 125, meaning she needs 125 particles). Assuming that the Energy Regen% is a multiplier (so that 200% ER means 2 particles are generated whenever 1 would normally be generated), we can know the particles generated in a rotation.

Taking Carlotta’s example specifically, her recommended ER amount is 115 to 125% (sourced from Prydwen.gg), and her Resonance cost is 125. If she generates X particles in her rotation and assuming 125% total ER as the safe amount, we have \(X*125\% = 125\), meaning she generates 100 particles. Zhezhi’s ability reads like a flat restoration of 15 particles, unaffected by ER. This reduces her Resonance Cost to 110 particles. Given that she generates 100 particles, we have \(100*Y\% = 110\), where Y is the new total resonance energy required. This is simply 110, meaning 110% ER is the safe ER required with Zhezhi.

In general, for a character with the Resonance Cost of RC, a flat reduction of X from outros, and the initial ER requirements of iER, the new ER requirements, nER can be found by (equating particles generated per rotation):

\[\frac{RC}{iER} = \frac{RC - X}{nER} \rightarrow nER = iER - \frac{X}{RC}iER\]

This equation basically reads: the same percentage reduction occurs in the required ER as the percentage reduction in Resonance Cost.
